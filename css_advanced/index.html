F.A.Q.

How does this work?

Once you sign up for our platform, you’ll get access to a wide range of tutorials and lessons curated by professional smile coaches. These lessons are structured to help you progress from beginner to advanced techniques in a fun and engaging way. You can track your progress, earn badges, and even get feedback from instructors.

How do I register?

Registration is easy! Just click on the "Register for Free" button found throughout the page. Fill in your basic details, choose a username and password, and you’ll be ready to start smiling. We don’t ask for any credit card or payment upfront — it’s totally free to get started.

How much does it cost?

Our basic membership is completely free and gives you access to all beginner-level tutorials and community support. If you want to unlock premium features, advanced lessons, and one-on-one sessions with smile experts, you can explore our affordable upgrade options — but there's never any pressure.

Do I need any prior experience?

Not at all. Our tutorials are designed for absolute beginners as well as experienced smilers looking to refine their technique. Each course includes step-by-step videos and guidance to help you improve at your own pace. Just bring your enthusiasm — we’ll take care of the rest.
